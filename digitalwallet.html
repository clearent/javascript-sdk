Digital Wallets Overview

A Digital Wallet allows Users to store their card securely on the platform of their choice. Customers might visit your site from one of these platforms and by giving them access to their stored cards you will contribute to a better user experience. We support the following:

  • Apple Pay For Web
  • Google Pay
